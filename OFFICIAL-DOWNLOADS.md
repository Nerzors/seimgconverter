## ✅ Allowed sharing
You may share **links** to the official sources above.

## ❌ Not allowed
Without prior written permission, you may **not**:
- re-upload, mirror, or host the software anywhere (including “backup” mirrors)
- bundle the software with other installers or software packs
- offer downloads via third-party sites, forums, Discord attachments, cloud drives, torrents, etc.
- redistribute modified builds, repacked archives, or “pre-configured” versions
- distribute any part of the software or extracted components

## Reporting unofficial downloads
If you find unofficial/mirrored downloads, please open an issue (or contact us) with:
- the link/source
- a short description (where it appears)
